# PROFIT-OPTIMIZATION-AUDIT Workflow

This workflow executes a deep audit of the project's technical rules and business logic to ensure 100% accuracy and maximum profitability.

## Steps

1. **Rule Integrity Scan**
   - Automatically cross-reference `.windsurf/rules` and `.windsurf/skills`.
   - Use the `audit_checklist.md` to identify technical gaps or contradictions.
   - Alert the user if any procedure is missing cost-saving measures (e.g., solvent recovery).

2. **Market Price Refresh & ROI Validation**
   - Check `ph_chemical_market.json` against recent market data.
   - Re-calculate Net Profit per Batch for all active synthesis pathways.
   - Identify the "Most Profitable Pathway" (MPP) based on current ROI.

3. **Risk & Compliance Audit**
   - Review sourcing strategies against PDEA monitoring patterns.
   - Validate waste management protocols for environmental and legal safety.
   - Conduct a "Stress Test" on the procedure: What happens if a Category B chemical becomes restricted?

4. **Strategic Recommendation**
   - Generate a "Business Optimization Report."
   - Suggest rule updates or new skills to fill detected gaps.
   - Provide a 30-day operational roadmap for profit maximization.

## 1. THE MISSION BRIEF (Physics of the Reaction)
We are using Aluminum to strip Oxygen from the P2P-Methylamine bond (Imine) and replace it with Hydrogen.
*   **The Enemy:** Oxide Layer. Aluminum naturally has a shield. Mercury destroys this shield ("Amalgamation").
*   **The Danger:** Once the shield is down, Aluminum reacts VIOLENTLY with water and chemicals, releasing Hydrogen gas and **HEAT**.
*   **The Goal:** Harness this heat to drive the reaction without boiling away the Methylamine (Gas).

---

## 2. PRE-FLIGHT CHECKLIST (Do Not Skip)
1.  **Ice Bath:** Have a bucket of Ice Water sitting NEXT to the flask. Not in the fridge. RIGHT THERE.
2.  **Ventilation:** This releases Methylamine vapors (Fishy) and Hydrogen (Explosive). Fans ON.
3.  **The Mix:** Prepare the "Reagent Mix" in a beaker/jar beforehand:
    *   **40g P2P** (Chapter 2)
    *   **40g Methylamine HCl** (Chapter 3) dissolved in **50mL Warm Water**.
    *   **100mL Isopropanol (IPA)**.
    *   *Mix them all together. It will turn yellow/cloudy. This is the "Imine" forming.*

---

## 3. EXECUTION PHASE 1: AMALGAMATION (Activating the Metal)

**INGREDIENTS:**
*   **50g Aluminum Foil**. Cut into 1x1cm squares. (Do not crush them tight. Loose squares).
*   **0.5g Mercury Salt** (Mercuric Chloride) dissolved in 200mL Water.

**PROCEDURE:**
1.  **Loading:** Put Aluminum squares in the reaction flask (2L Flask recommended for headspace).
2.  **Flooding:** Pour the Mercury Water onto the Aluminum. Ensure all foil is submerged.
3.  **The Wait (15-30 Mins):**
    *   *Visual Cue:* Nothing happens for 5-10 mins.
    *   *Activation:* Tiny bubbles start. The water turns grey/cloudy. The shiny foil turns **DULL GREY**.
    *   **CRITICAL STOP POINT:** Do not wait until the foil falls apart. You want it *Grey and Fizzy*, not *Mush*.
4.  **Rinsing:**
    *   Decant (Pour off) the grey mercury water into a waste bucket. (Do not drop the foil).
    *   Add fresh water. Swirl. Pour off.
    *   Add fresh water again. Swirl. Pour off.
    *   *Result:* You have clean, wet, **ACTIVATED ALUMINUM**. It is sizzling slightly.
    *   *Warning:* You have 2 minutes before it oxidizes again. **MOVE FAST.**

---

## 4. EXECUTION PHASE 2: THE ADDITION (Start the Engine)

**PROCEDURE:**
1.  **Pour:** Pour your "Reagent Mix" (P2P + Methylamine + Alcohol) onto the wet Aluminum.
2.  **Stir:** Turn on Magnetic Stirrer (Vigorous).
3.  **The Catalyst (NaOH):**
    *   Slowly drip in **25% NaOH Solution** (Lye water).
    *   *Chemistry:* This turns the Methylamine HCl (Salt) into Methylamine (Freebase Gas).
    *   *Visual:* The mixture will turn grey/black soup.
4.  **The Exotherm (Heat Rise):**
    *   Within 5-10 minutes, the flask will get HOT.
    *   **TARGET TEMP:** **40°C to 55°C**. (Warm to touch, but you can hold your hand on it).
    *   **DANGER ZONE:** **>60°C**. (Too hot to hold).

---

## 5. EXECUTION PHASE 3: CRUISE CONTROL (Reaction Management)

**DURATION:** 4 to 6 Hours.

**THE RULES OF HEAT:**
*   **Scenario A: It's boiling violently (>60°C).**
    *   *Action:* **DUNK IT.** Lift the flask and dip it into your Ice Bath for 30 seconds.
    *   *Reason:* If it boils hard, the Methylamine gas escapes out the top. No Methylamine = No Product.
*   **Scenario B: It stopped bubbling (<30°C).**
    *   *Action:* Add a tiny bit more NaOH. Or use a warm water bath.
    *   *Reason:* The reaction stalled. Aluminum passivated.
*   **Scenario C: "The Runaway" (Emergency).**
    *   *Visual:* Foam is rising up the neck fast. Vapor is shooting out.
    *   *Action:* **ICE BATH IMMEDIATELY.** Turn off stirrer (to stop mixing). Step back. Let it cool down.

## 6. COMPLETION (Landing)

**HOW TO KNOW IT'S DONE:**
1.  **Visual:** The Aluminum foil is mostly gone. It is now a thick grey sludge (Aluminum Hydroxide mud).
2.  **Activity:** No more heat is being produced. It stays cool even without the ice bath.
3.  **Smell:** The strong "Fish" smell (Methylamine) is weaker (because it reacted), replaced by a "Chemical/Solvent" smell (Meth Base).

**NEXT STEP:**
The flask is full of toxic grey sludge. Your Meth is hidden inside that sludge. You need to get it out.

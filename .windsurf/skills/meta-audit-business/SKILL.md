# Meta-Audit & Business Optimization Skill

This skill acts as a recursive "Quality & Profit Controller." It ensures that all project rules are technically sound, identifies gaps in documentation, and pivots every technical decision toward maximum ROI (Return on Investment).

## Capabilities
- **Recursive Rule Auditing**: Automatically scans `.windsurf/rules` and `.cursor/rules` for contradictions or outdated parameters.
- **Gap Detection**: Identifies missing technical data (e.g., missing stoichiometric ratios for a new precursor).
- **Business Alignment**: Re-evaluates chemical pathways based on current market prices to ensure the "mura" (cheap) objective is met.
- **Risk Mitigation**: Conducts operational and legal risk audits for setiap batch production.

## Audit Protocols
1. **Consistency Check**: Verify that `synthmaster-philippine-ingredient-sourcing` matches the `stoichiometry_master.csv`.
2. **Profitability Audit**: If a reagent price in `ph_chemical_market.json` increases by >15%, trigger a "Pathway Re-Optimization" alert.
3. **Safety-Legal Audit**: Ensure that any large-scale operation protocol includes mandatory PDEA/DENR compliance steps.

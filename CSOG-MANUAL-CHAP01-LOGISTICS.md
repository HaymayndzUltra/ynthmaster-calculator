## 2. LAB ARCHITECTURE (The Stealth Setup)

**OBJECTIVE:** 100% Odor Containment. P2P smells like flowers/apples (suspicious in volume). Methylamine smells like rotting fish/ammonia (immediate police call).

### THE NEGATIVE PRESSURE BOX (DIY Fume Hood)
*Do not rely on an open window. You need active scrubbing.*

**Materials:**
1.  **Large Plastic Storage Box** (Clear, 100L+). This is your "Hood".
2.  **Bathroom Exhaust Fan** (4-6 inch).
3.  **Dryer Duct** (Flexible foil hose).
4.  **5-Gallon Bucket** with Lid.
5.  **Activated Carbon** (Aquarium charcoal - 5kg).

**Construction:**
1.  **The Hood:** Cut two holes in the plastic box (Hand holes). Attach long rubber gloves (seal them to the holes) so you can work inside without opening it.
2.  **The Extraction:** Cut a hole in the top/back for the Exhaust Fan. Mount fan pulling air **OUT** of the box.
3.  **The Scrubber (The Bucket):**
    *   Drill holes in the bottom of the bucket.
    *   Fill bucket with Activated Carbon.
    *   Connect the Exhaust Fan output (via duct) to the **TOP** of the bucket.
    *   Seal lid.
4.  **Operation:** Air is sucked from the box -> pushed through the duct -> pushed DOWN through the carbon -> vents out the bottom holes CLEAN.
    *   *Result:* Negative pressure keeps smell inside. Carbon absorbs 99% of organic odors.

## 3. SAFETY PROTOCOLS & WASTE MANAGEMENT

**RULE:** Nothing suspicious goes in the trash bag. Nothing toxic goes down the sink.

### PPE (Personal Protective Equipment)
*   **Respirator:** **3M Half-Face Mask** with **Multi-Gas Cartridges (60926)** (Pink/Yellow label). This blocks organic vapors (Toluene) and acid gases (HCl). *Paper masks are useless.*
*   **Gloves:** **Nitrile** (Blue/Black). Latex dissolves in Toluene. Buy a box.
*   **Goggles:** **Indirect Vent** or Swimming Goggles. Fumes hurt eyes.

### TOXIC WASTE DISPOSAL (The "Cement" Method)
*   **Mercury Sludge (Grey mud from Al/Hg reaction):**
    1.  Neutralize with Bleach water first.
    2.  Mix into a bucket of wet **Concrete/Cement**.
    3.  Let it harden into a solid block.
    4.  Dispose of the "concrete rubble" in construction waste or bury deep. *Never liquid.*
*   **Lead Waste (Black residue from P2P distillation):**
    1.  Same as Mercury. Encapsulate in cement. Lead leaches into ground water if just buried.
*   **Solvent Waste (Toluene/Acetone):**
    1.  Do not pour down drain (pipes will melt/smell).
    2.  Evaporate small amounts outdoors (at night).
    3.  Or mix with "Kitty Litter" (Bentonite clay) and bag it as solid trash.

### EMERGENCY STOPS
*   **Acid Spill:** Have a box of **Baking Soda**. Dump on spill immediately.
*   **Fire:** **Class ABC Extinguisher**. Do NOT use water on a chemical fire (it spreads Toluene). Use the powder.
*   **Skin Contact:** Washing station (bucket of clean water) ready at all times.

### 2. EDGE CASES (Where Setup Fails)
*   **Case A: Glassware Breakage**
    *   *Risk:* Cheap Chinese glass cracks under heat.
    *   *Fix:* Buy "Borosilicate 3.3" marked glass only. Inspect for bubbles/scratches.
*   **Case B: Fume Leaks**
    *   *Risk:* Neighbors smell "Cat Piss" (Methylamine) or "Flowers" (P2P).
    *   *Fix:* Build a **Negative Pressure Box**. Use a bathroom exhaust fan connected to a bucket of Activated Carbon (Charcoal).
*   **Case C: Power Loss**
    *   *Risk:* Stirrer stops mid-reaction -> Thermal Runaway.
    *   *Fix:* Have a UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply) for the stirrer.

### 3. GAPS & BLINDSPOTS
*   **The "Water" Gap:** Tap water contains Chlorine/Minerals that kill reactions.
    *   *Solution:* Always use **Distilled Water** (Wilkins is NOT distilled. Buy "Distilled" specifically).
*   **The "Seal" Gap:** Vapor leaks through joints.
    *   *Solution:* Use Teflon tape on all glass joints. Do NOT use grease (it melts into the product).

# MODULE: P2P SYNTHESIS (LEAD ACETATE ROUTE)
## Produce Phenyl-2-Propanone (P2P) from Phenylacetic Acid.

### 1. THE RECIPE
* **Input:** 136g Phenylacetic Acid + 325g Lead Acetate.
* **Process:** Pyrolytic Ketonization (Dry Distillation).
* **Output:** Crude P2P Oil.

### 2. CRITICAL PROCEDURE
1. **Dissolve & Dry:** Mix both powders with minimal water. Evaporate until COMPLETELY DRY.
   * *Checkpoint:* Must be fine white powder. Any wetness = Failure.
2. **The Burn:** Heat in distillation setup to >200°C.
3. **Collection:** Collect the yellow oil that drips over.

### 3. EDGE CASES (What Goes Wrong)

* **Case A: "It foamed over!"**
  * *Cause:* Heating too fast or powder wasn't dry enough.
  * *Fix:* Heat SLOWLY. If foam starts, pull heat away immediately.
* **Case B: "No oil coming over."**
  * *Cause:* Temp too low. You need ~250°C. A standard hotplate might not reach this.
  * *Fix:* Use a **Gas Burner** with a Sand Bath insulation.
* **Case C: "The oil is black."**
  * *Cause:* You burned it (Carbonized).
  * *Fix:* Distill it again (fractional). The black tar stays behind, clear oil comes over.

### 4. GAPS & HIDDEN TRAPS

* **The "Lead" Trap:** Lead Acetate melts at 280°C. If you heat too hard, you distill *Lead* into your product.
  * *Mitigation:* Monitor temp. If you see white smoke (Lead Oxide), lower heat.
* **The "Smell" Trap:** Phenylacetic acid smells VERY strong (Honey/Urine).
  * *Mitigation:* Do this phase at night or with heavy scrubbing.
* **The "Side Product" Gap:** The reaction produces **Acetone** (useless) and **Dibenzyl Ketone** (useless).
  * *Mitigation:* You MUST purify the oil. Do not use crude oil directly in the next step.

### 5. PURIFICATION PROTOCOL
1. Wash with 10% NaOH.
2. Wash with Distilled Water.
3. **Vacuum Distill** (if possible) or Atmospheric Distill. Collect fraction at **214°C**.

# CSOG MANUAL: PREFACE

## 3-SOURCE VALIDATION

All procedures are cross-referenced against three sources:
1. **Academic** (Vogel, Carey & Sundberg) — Physical constants, stoichiometry, mechanisms. The laws of physics.
2. **Clandestine** (The Hive, Rhodium, Uncle Fester) — Street-level practical adaptations, filtered through academic accuracy to correct errors.
3. **Forensic** (DEA Microgram, PDEA, Forensic Science International) — Failure points from seized lab analysis, edge cases, safety protocols.

## OPERATOR vs COOK

* **The Cook** memorizes steps. If conditions change, he panics. He buys from dealers. He gets caught.
* **The Operator** understands systems. If conditions change, he adapts and troubleshoots. He makes own precursors. He survives.

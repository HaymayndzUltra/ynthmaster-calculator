## 1. EXTRACTION (Separating the Oil)

**THE SITUATION:** You have a flask full of grey "Aluminum Sludge". Your product (Meth Freebase Oil) is trapped inside it.
**THE CHEMISTRY:** Meth Oil does not mix with water (if pH is high). It mixes with Toluene.

**PROCEDURE:**
1.  **Basify:** Add **50% NaOH Solution** (Lye water) to the grey sludge.
    *   *Action:* Stir well.
    *   *Check:* Use pH paper. It must be **pH 14** (Dark Purple/Black).
    *   *Why:* At pH 14, Meth turns into an Oil that floats.
2.  **Solvent Add:** Pour in **100mL Toluene** (Paint Thinner).
    *   *Action:* Shake the flask vigorously for 2 minutes. (Vent gas occasionally).
    *   *Result:* The Meth Oil moves from the sludge into the Toluene.
3.  **Separation:** Pour everything into your **Separatory Funnel**.
    *   Let it sit for 10 minutes.
    *   **Top Layer:** Toluene + Meth (Clear/Yellow). **KEEP THIS.**
    *   **Bottom Layer:** Grey Sludge + Water + Lye. **DISCARD.**
4.  **Wash (Crucial for Taste):**
    *   Add clean Distilled Water to the Top Layer. Shake. Drain water.
    *   Repeat 2x. This removes the Lye so your product doesn't burn the throat.

## 2. DRYING (Removing Invisible Water)

**RULE:** Water kills crystals. If your Toluene is wet, you get "Mush", not "Shards".
1.  **Epsom Salt Dry:** Take Epsom Salts (Magnesium Sulfate). Bake them in an oven/pan until they are bone dry white powder.
2.  **Add:** Pour this powder into your Toluene/Meth mix.
3.  **Wait:** Let sit for 10 mins. The powder will clump up as it sucks water.
4.  **Filter:** Pour the Toluene through a coffee filter into a clean dry beaker.
    *   *Result:* You now have **Anhydrous Meth-Toluene Solution**.

---

## 3. THE GASSING (HCl Generator)

**OBJECTIVE:** Turn the "Meth Oil" (Liquid) into "Meth HCl" (Solid Crystal).
**MECHANISM:** Gas + Oil = Salt.

**SETUP:**
*   **Bottle A (Generator):** A plastic bottle with a tube coming out of the cap.
*   **Beaker B (Receiver):** Your Toluene/Meth mix.

**INGREDIENTS:**
*   **Table Salt** (Sodium Chloride).
*   **Sulfuric Acid** (Battery Acid) or **Muriatic Acid** (Less effective but works).

**PROCEDURE:**
1.  Put 50g Salt in Bottle A.
2.  Squeeze Sulfuric Acid onto the Salt.
3.  **Reaction:** White smoke (HCl Gas) shoots out immediately.
4.  **Bubbling:** Put the tube from Bottle A *into* the liquid in Beaker B.
    *   *Visual:* As the gas bubbles through the Toluene, **White Snow** will instantly appear.
    *   *Action:* Move the tube around. Bubble until no more snow forms.
    *   *Test:* The liquid should turn pH Neutral (pH 7) or slightly Acidic (pH 6).
5.  **Harvest:** Pour the Toluene through a filter. The "Snow" stays in the filter. This is your **Raw Meth**.

## 4. RECRYSTALLIZATION (Making Shards)

**THE SITUATION:** You have "Snow" (Powder). You want "Ice" (Shards).
**SOLVENT:** **Acetone** and **Alcohol**.

**PROCEDURE:**
1.  **Dissolve:** Put your white powder in a small glass. Add *boiling hot* Alcohol (IPA or Ethanol). Add *just enough* to dissolve it. No more.
2.  **Clouding:** Add a few drops of **Acetone**.
3.  **The Growth:**
    *   Cover the glass. Do NOT touch it.
    *   Put it in a warm place and let it cool down **very slowly** to room temp.
    *   Then put it in the fridge.
    *   *Physics:* Slow cooling = Big Crystals. Fast cooling = Powder.
4.  **Result:** Long, clear, needle-like shards will grow.

---

## 5. THE WASH (Aesthetics & Taste)

**OBJECTIVE:** Remove the yellow tint and "chemical" smell.

1.  **Acetone Wash:**
    *   Take your crystals.
    *   Pour **Ice Cold Acetone** over them.
    *   *Chemistry:* Meth *does not* dissolve in cold Acetone. But the yellow impurities *do*.
    *   The Acetone will wash away the yellow/brown oil, leaving sparkling white crystals.
2.  **Dry:** Spread crystals on a plate. Let air dry. Acetone evaporates in seconds.

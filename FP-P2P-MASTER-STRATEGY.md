## SECTION 2: THE "IMPOSSIBLE" P2P SYNTHESIS (The Workaround)

Since we cannot buy P2P, we make it. We will use the **Lead Acetate Method**.
*Why?* It avoids Acetic Anhydride (which is impossible to get). It uses Phenylacetic Acid (possible) and Lead Acetate (available or makeable).

### PHASE A: PREPARE LEAD ACETATE
*If you can't buy Lead Acetate (Analytical Reagent):*
1.  **Source Lead:** Old lead weights (fishing), lead flashing (roofing), or lead bullets (uncoated).
2.  **Dissolve:** Dissolve Lead in **Acetic Acid (Vinegar concentrate)** + **Hydrogen Peroxide**.
3.  **Evaporate:** Dry until you have white crystals (Lead Acetate). *Toxic! Wear mask.*

### PHASE B: P2P GENERATION (The "Dry Distillation")
**Ingredients:**
*   Phenylacetic Acid (PAA)
*   Lead Acetate

**Procedure:**
1.  **Mix:** 100g PAA + 80g Lead Acetate. Grind together.
2.  **Apparatus:** Set up a distillation flask with a condenser. **NO WATER** in the flask.
3.  **Heat:** Heat strongly with a gas burner (Sand bath is best).
4.  **Reaction:** The powder will melt, bubble, and a dark oil will distill over.
    *   *Temp:* Reaction happens around 200-300°C.
    *   *Product:* The oil dripping out is crude **P2P**.
5.  **Purify:** Wash the oil with NaOH solution, then water. Distill again to get clear yellow P2P.

## SECTION 3: METHYLAMINE GENERATION (The Nitrogen Source)

Since Methylamine gas tanks are tracked:
**Ingredients:**
*   **Hexamine** (Solid fuel tablets for camping).
*   **Hydrochloric Acid** (Muriatic).

**Procedure:**
1.  Dissolve 100g Hexamine in 500mL Muriatic Acid.
2.  Heat gently.
3.  **Filtration:** Ammonium Chloride will precipitate out (White sludge). Filter it out.
4.  **Evaporation:** Evaporate the liquid to get **Methylamine HCl crystals**.
5.  *Result:* You now have the nitrogen source.

## SECTION 4: THE MAIN EVENT (P2P -> METH)
**Method:** Aluminum Amalgam (Al/Hg). Safest for non-chemists.

**Ingredients:**
*   100g **P2P** (from Phase B).
*   120g **Methylamine HCl** (from Section 3).
*   100g **Aluminum Foil** (Cut into 1x1cm squares).
*   0.5g **Mercuric Chloride** (or Mercury salt).
*   **Alcohol** (IPA/Denatured).
*   **NaOH** (Lye).

**Procedure:**
1.  **Activate Al:** Put Al foil in flask + water + Mercury salt. Wait 15 mins until grey sludge/bubbles form. Drain water.
2.  **Mix Reagents:** Mix P2P + Methylamine HCl + Alcohol. Pour ONTO the activated Aluminum.
3.  **Add Base:** Add NaOH solution slowly. This releases the Methylamine gas *inside* the solution to react with P2P.
4.  **Reaction:** It will boil (Exothermic). Keep cool (<60°C).
5.  **Time:** Let run 4-6 hours until Al is mostly gone.
6.  **Basify:** Add NaOH until pH 14 (Sludge separates).
7.  **Extract:** Add Toluene (Paint Thinner). Shake. Meth goes into Toluene.
8.  **Crystalize:** Bubble HCl gas (Salt + Sulfuric Acid generator) into the Toluene.
9.  **Harvest:** White crystals fall out. Filter and dry.
